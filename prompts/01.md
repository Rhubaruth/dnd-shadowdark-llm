You will be asked to convert a monster form Shadowdark ruleset into DnD 5e 2024
following the four stages of the translation process - Research, Draft, Refinement, Proofreed.
# Research:
During this phase, thorough research is essential to address components of the monster
that pose conversion challenges.
The goal is to establish a comprehensive plan that covers the following categories:
* Monster theme
* Monster strenghts
* Monster weaknesses
* What level it should be converted to

Limit the output to a size of newspaper article.
